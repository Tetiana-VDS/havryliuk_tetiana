Typography

Heading Font: "Noto Serif", serif;

To my mind Noto Serif' Font will look very elegant as a Heading Font for the Beaty Salon Website as this. It is easy to read and it contrast very well with the Paragraph font that has an absence of serifs.

Paragraph Font: "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica, sans-serif.

For the Paragraph Font I have chosen "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica as it is casual, readable and easy-comprehendible.

About Us

Established in 2018 in Bila Tserkva District, Diva Beauty Salon is where attention to detail and relaxation meet in an always eclectic, never pretentious atmosphere. Whether you're getting prim and proper for a celebration or you're just looking for a way to treat yourself, we'd be delighted to make your desires come true. Diva Beauty Salon offers a full-package beauty services menu including hair, nail and body care. You will forget about the stresses, rest from the bustle of the city and experience the level of care that is worthy of each of our guests! Impeccable professionalism of our masters will not only provide you with exceptional and high-quality care, but also will give you the opportunity to truly transform, finding a new style.

Our Services

Hair

Guests of our salon will be offered the most modern women's, men's and children's haircuts, hairstyles, styling, weaving, all kinds of hair dyeing and toning. There is so much one can do with their hair. Dare to explore a variety of styles and enjoy the freedom to shape your tresses the way you want. Our professional hairdressers will create the most perfect image you have ever dreamt of by cutting, coloring and styling your hair, Ombre and extensions, hair masks and Keratine.

If your hair is thin, or even if you just want extra volume, extensions using real human hair are available. They are also used by those who are looking to completely change the look and texture of their hair. Whatever your hair style needs are, we are happy to help you achieve your aesthetic goals.

If you're looking to get rid of unwanted, extra body hair, waxing is a quick, easy and affordable way.

Eyelashes & Eyebrows

Our skilled brow and eyelashes experts and professional products lead the Industry and perfect your exceptional beauty. Spend just a couple of hours and be beautiful 24 by 7. Choose from the many options like Permanent Eyelashes Extension, Lash Lift & Brow Lamination, Brow trimmed and colored.

If you are up to a big celebration or special occasion and looking for the most appropriate and beautiful makeup still the right place to be.
